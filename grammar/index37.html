Prepositions of Place — місцезнаходження

in, on, under, next to, between, opposite, behind, in front of

ПрийменникПриклад
in — в/уThe keys are in the bag.
on — наThe book is on the table.
under — підThe cat is under the chair.
next to — поручHe sits next to me.
between — міжThe bank is between the café and the park.
opposite — навпротиThe school is opposite the museum.
behind / in front ofThe car is behind the house / in front of the house.
